A guide that walks with you

Connect a dxFeed data feed.

The progress rail fills as you scroll; tick the checks as you go — the page remembers where you are. (Full article library ships alongside this experience.)

1

Sign your market data agreement

Bought on the DeepCharts website? Sign in your dashboard — Subscriptions → Sign Market Data Contract. Nothing streams until this exists. Bought on dxfeed.com? Sign there, and make sure the platform selected is DeepChart.

✓Agreement signed and confirmed
2

Enter your credentials in Feed Settings

Open Feed Settings, choose Source dxFeed, and paste the username and password from your dxFeed welcome email — not your DeepCharts account login.

✓Credentials saved without errors
3

Map your exchanges in Symbol Manage

Options → Symbol Manage decides which feed powers which exchange. The instrument picker only shows what you subscribe here — an empty picker means nothing is mapped.

✓CME (or your exchange) mapped to dxFeed
4

Connect and watch the dot

Hit connect. The feed badge in the top bar turns green when live. Red means disconnected; if charts still don't move, the market may be closed or the symbol unmapped.

✓Green dot, ticks flowing
Verified working: a chart on ES prints live candles and the DOM ladder updates. Something failed? The troubleshooting web catches every known dxFeed error — DLL, DATA_ACCESS_SUSPENDED, bad server — one link away.
